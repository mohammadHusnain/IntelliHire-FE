# AI Interview & Recruitment Platform

An AI-powered interview preparation, recruitment, and talent community platform designed for both **individual users** and **companies**.

The platform combines:

* AI-based mock interview practice
* Smart recruitment & candidate screening
* Long-term talent community building

It helps candidates improve their interview skills while enabling companies to streamline hiring through AI-assisted workflows and targeted talent networks.

---

# Overview

This platform is built around three major goals:

1. **AI Mock Interview Preparation** for individual users
2. **AI-Assisted Recruitment & Hiring** for companies
3. **Talent Community Building** for long-term engagement

The system assists companies in making better hiring decisions while keeping final recruitment decisions under human control.

---

# Core Features

## Individual User Features

The platform allows individual users to prepare for interviews through intelligent AI-powered mock interview experiences.

### Features

* User registration & authentication
* Profile creation & management
* CV/Resume upload
* Role-specific interview selection
* AI-powered mock interviews
* Real-time interview interaction
* Performance analysis & feedback

### AI Feedback Includes

* Communication quality
* Confidence analysis
* Technical relevance
* Strengths & weaknesses
* Improvement suggestions
* Interview readiness insights

### Goal

Help users improve interview performance through practice, feedback, and self-assessment.

# Candidate Application Flow

Candidates can:

* Open shared application links
* Submit personal information
* Upload CV/Resume
* Apply for job opportunities

All candidate data is automatically stored and managed inside the system database.

---

# AI Hiring Pipeline

After applications are submitted, the platform initiates the AI-assisted hiring workflow.

## 1. CV Analysis

The system analyzes uploaded CVs and extracts information such as:

* Skills
* Education
* Experience
* Projects
* Certifications
* Technical keywords

The extracted data is then compared against job requirements.

### Output

* Candidate relevance scoring
* Shortlisting recommendations
* Job-fit analysis

---

## 2. AI Screening Interview

Shortlisted candidates proceed to an initial AI screening interview.

### Screening Evaluation Areas

* Communication skills
* Motivation
* Basic role understanding
* Personality indicators
* Initial suitability assessment

### Purpose

Perform early-stage candidate filtering before technical evaluation.

---

## 3. AI Technical Interview

Candidates who pass screening move to a more advanced technical interview stage.

### Technical Interview Focus

* Role-specific questioning
* Skill-based assessment
* Job description alignment
* CV-based personalized questions
* Technical competency evaluation

### Goal

Assess deeper job-fit and technical capability.

---

# Candidate Evaluation Reports

After interview stages are completed, the system generates structured candidate evaluation reports.

## Reports Include

* CV match score
* Screening interview performance
* Technical interview performance
* Strengths & weaknesses
* AI-generated recommendations
* Overall candidate evaluation

These reports are displayed inside the recruiter/HR dashboard for final human review and decision-making.

# AI-Assisted, Not Fully Automated

The platform is designed to assist and improve recruitment workflows through AI.

### Important Principle

* AI supports screening and evaluation
* Human recruiters make the final hiring decisions

This ensures:

* Better efficiency
* Reduced manual workload
* Smarter candidate filtering
* Human-controlled recruitment outcomes
